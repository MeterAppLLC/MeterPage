Meter App

Create your own ride share network

Who we are.

We are the creators of Meter for Uber. Its an app that lets you measure the cost of your Uber or Lyft ride while you're in it.

Where we want to go

We are hard at work building software that lets anyone create their own rideshare network. With our product you will be able to set your prices. It will be your business. Your rules.

Features

  • Custom app icon for your business.
  • Ability to set the prices you want to charge.
  • Schedule rides ahead of time or be available on demand
  • Consumer and driver Apps for Android and iPhone
  • Gather useful data about each ride you or your fleet gives and analyze efficiency.
  • Accept Tips
  • We take care of payments. Riders have the same great payment experience they get with uber or Lyft.
  • Keep your money. Unlike Uber we charge upfront for the software and then only what we need to maintain servers and keep your app up to date. No more losing 20% of your revenue to The Man.
